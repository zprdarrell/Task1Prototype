Hungry or thirsty? We've got you covered! With a variety of restaurants and bars, you're sure to find something to suit your taste.

Important Notes:

  • The drinking age is 18 years old but is not strictly enforced.
  • Last call for alcohol is 12:00am and will not resume service until 9:00am the next morning.
  • Violent crime is rare in Taniti, but please drink responsibly as there have been reports of pickpocketing and other petty crimes.
